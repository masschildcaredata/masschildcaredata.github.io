Massachusetts Early Education and Care Data

This is data from the early care and education program search site provided by the Massachusetts Early Education and Childcare.

In Massachusetts, there are no licensed childcare providers that are non-educational. Thus, essentially, that site contains information about all of the licensed childcare providers in Mass.
